[raveStats]

Diagram shows daily statistics of RAVE survey observations collected from the publicly available Data Release 4. Each column represents a single year, each box a single day. Colors correspond to the values of the parameters listed on the right. Whenever applicable, averages or medians (indicated by M) of indiviual measurement are shown. It's easy to see how the radial velocity varies on a monthly basis and that mostly brighter stars were observed in 2008. Hovering over the box shows the parameter value and clicking on it will display its contents.
